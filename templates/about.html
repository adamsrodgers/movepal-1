About Movepal

When moving to a new city, you want to find out everything you can from properties, to nearby schools, and so much more that you look for in a new area. And when you look at a new area, do you often find yourself having so many tabs open in your web browser to look at all of the things you want to look at? Movepal has the solution for you as a one stop place to find everything you need to know when moving to a new city!

Authors: Nahom, Tinsae, Terris, and Raekwon
